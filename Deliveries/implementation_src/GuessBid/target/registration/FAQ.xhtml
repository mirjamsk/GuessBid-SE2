What is inverse auction?

Inverse auctio also known as lowest unique bid auction, is a type of strategy game related to traditional auction. With one difference, the winner is the individual with the lowest unique bid.

How do I deposit credit into my account?

At this point the application does not provide an interface to deposit money and therefore update your credit. Users can transfer money via a regular bank transaction to our company's account. After the money has been received, the system administrators will update user's credit accordingly

Why van't I edit my auction?

Sellers cannot edit details of an auction once it has been created. This is to protect the bidders of an auction. ( The users place bids on a, and because of a specific description. It should not be the case that they place a bid on one thing and then win the auction for a variant, or worse, completely different thing). Sellers can however delete the auction should they need to.

How is shipment/exchange handled?

GuessBid does not provide or organize the shipment or any other kind of item exchange. After an auction is finished, the winner and seller receive notifications containing each others emails. The two parties are expected to come to mutually satisfying agreement and handle the exchange privately and fairly.
